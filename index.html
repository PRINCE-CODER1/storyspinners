LET'S GET PUBLISHED!

Your books undergo several phases before we make it open
for the world to see it, and we want you to read each of those
phases before you can get your book published with us. Read
below!

What are the requirements needed to get published with us?


First, you need to choose your most desired package according to your needs.
Second, in the manuscript details section you will be required to specify the following:
•Book name
•Author Name
•Genre
•About The Book
•About the Author
•Author Pic

What will happen after you register?


Our team will contact you as soon as you register yourself with us, and will verify all the details submitted by you while registering.
Once everything is sorted, we will provide you with your personal Authors Dashboard within 24 hours, where you can add your book, and fill in its details like author name, description, sample of cover & interior etc.

What is the approximate time needed to get published?

After final submission of your book and your approval, we need an approximate time period of 25-30 days in order to bring the book into the market.

How to track royalty after book is published?


You can track the royalties earned from your book directly from your Dashboard.

Last but not not the least,


-After you have submitted your book file successfully, our team shall contact you within 48 hours.
-We dont charge any extra hidden charges.
-We provide discounts on book sales, as per your requests.
- There are no hidden charges involved in our Authors Dashboard.
